# Credit Card Campaign Analytics: Sign-Up Success and Spending Trends
This project focuses on assessing the performance of credit card campaigns by measuring customer acquisition (sign-ups) and changes in spending patterns following promotional activities. 
Through data analysis, it aims to identify high-performing segments and recommend strategies to optimize campaign effectiveness and revenue growth.

**Objective :**

*  Evaluated credit card campaign success by analyzing customer sign-ups and spending behavior.
*  Identified key customer segments to improve targeting and campaign performance.
*  Delivered insights to optimize conversions and increase customer spending.
